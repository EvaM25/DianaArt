About me

With 6 year of experience as an event illustrator, I have brought over 2500 quick portraits to life. My preferred mediums are watercolor, ink, and markers, which allow me to infuse each sketch with vibrancy and character. Beyond live sketches, I have a passion for fashion illustration and offer custom artwork.

My creations are perfect for a range of uses, from advertising campaigns and elegant postcards to invitations, stylish T-shirt designs and covers. Whatever your artistic needs, I’m here to deliver illustrations that stand out.

Styles

With expertise spanning across multiple disciplines of event illustration, I am the perfect person to engage for comprehensive projects. No matter the scope of your requirements, I am fully equipped to tackle any creative endeavor with custom illustrations that will elevate your event. Click on the pictures to see more.

Classic style

Full-length figure sketches with a focus on outfit, hairstyle, and accessories. These sketches, complete with expressive faces, are perfect for family gatherings and corporate events. Each piece is meticulously drawn in just 8-9 minutes, using either vibrant watercolors or bold markers.

Portrait style

Detailed portrait sketches focusing on capturing likeness and emotion with special attention to accessories and makeup. Ideal for corporate events, anniversaries, and high-traffic gatherings. Each sketch takes 8-9 minutes to complete and can be done in watercolors or markers.

Fashion style

The fastest style sketch. Stylized full-body illustrations capture the essence of your look, focusing on outfit, hairstyle, and accessories while omitting facial details. Each piece is brought to life in just 6-7 minutes through the fluid grace of watercolors or the bold definition of markers.

Anime style

Perfect for capturing the adorable children’s face expressions at your child’s party. Each portrait, crafted with premium markers, is completed in just 8 minutes.»

B&W graphic style

Detailed close-up portraits capturing the exact likeness, meticulously crafted with black colored pencils and ink. Alternatively, you can choose markers for a bold, expressive finish. Your personalized portrait is ready in just 8 minutes.
